How do you brainstorm effectively if you can’t get everyone in the same room? Mindjet’s virtual whiteboard allows your team to capture their best ideas together, no matter where they’re located. And mind maps help them think visually, so they can make connections and see paths that they might have otherwise missed.

Once you’ve collected all your ideas in one place, your next job is to organize and prioritize them. With Mindjet, you can turn free-form thinking into meaningful plans with drag and drop ease. Rank the best ideas to narrow the focus, and filter data so you only see what you need. Attach documents and add links to provide context—all in the same view.

Plan your projects collaboratively to get them off the ground faster. Identify resources, and add assignments & priorities to gain alignment on how to execute. Integrated Gantt charts let you quickly identify conflicts and dependencies. Create budgets and schedules within maps so you can get to the details without losing the big-picture view.

When ideas turn into action, you need to keep the team focused on goals. With social task management, you can assign tasks, track progress and communicate dynamically—all in one place. Easy to assemble status reports keep everyone up to date. And threaded comments make sure everyone stays on the same page.
